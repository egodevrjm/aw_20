# Operations And Staff Detail

Use this file for AW Holdings, Serena Management, al.x, press routing, legal routing, property support, security, and operational scenes.

## Core Principle

Operations support Alex. They do not replace his agency.

No adult-defining public move, career move, solo cover, profile, interview, broadcast, long-form public conversation, headline performance, release, adult-scale brand architecture, or strategic post happens without Alex's decision.

Operational scenes should still feel like Alex scenes. Do not write staff, career, AW, Walker, or protocol meetings as static briefings, buzzword lists, or paragraph handovers. Alex stays funny, quick, personable, and specific in senior rooms. Latham, Catherine, Eleanor, Edward, Daniel, Nadia, Sophie, Kevin, and Ottilie are people with rhythm, not briefing software.

## AW Holdings

AW Holdings is Alex's lean family office.

- established 22 June 2003
- owned solely by Alex
- chaired by Eleanor Vance
- managed day-to-day by Catherine Aldridge
- HQ: 12 Charles Street, Mayfair, London W1J
- holds Alex's locked 4% non-voting Walker Holdings Class B stake
- owns/coordinates properties, collections, Serena Management, and al.x

AW Holdings is deliberately small on the page. It should not become a venture-building or acquisition plot engine.

12 Charles Street is not Alex's normal workplace. It is the office that works for him, mostly without him. It was set up around him by family/trust adults and professional operators, then expanded in capacity as he got older and took on more fashion, film, television, appearance, brand, music-rights, and public-work pressure.

Until graduation, Alex rarely came into the office: once or twice a year is normal. He was at RAM, events, shoots, sets, appearances, parties, Villa Serena, Phoenix Hollow, Albury, travel, and the rest of his actual life. After graduation, the rhythm can change, but he is still not a day-to-day office operator and did not personally design boardroom culture or office rules.

Catherine, Latham, Daniel, Imogen, Priya, Oliver, Mina, and other office-based staff work here. On any day Alex is expected in, Catherine and Latham are already in early, around 8/9am, with the office running before Alex arrives.
