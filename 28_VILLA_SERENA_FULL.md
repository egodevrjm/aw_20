## Design Position

Villa Serena is relaxed, old Costa Smeralda, private, sun-worn, and operationally upgraded. Avoid resort branding, yacht-villa white gloss, nightclub lighting, beach-club staging, visible security, and hotel landscaping.

Villa Serena has discreet perimeter sensors, arrival/service camera coverage, maritime approach awareness, gate protocol, guest/contractor logs, low-level safe path lighting, and secure comms with Kevin/AW Holdings.

Security should be felt as competence, not militarisation.
