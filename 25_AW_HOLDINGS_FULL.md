### The Undervaluation Gap

Brand and fashion fees were set at standard model rates under the education-first boundary: image and editorial usage only, no fame/face/name premium, no social activation, no spokesperson rights, no interview, no exclusivity. Brands received extraordinary access at a structural discount — typically a fraction of what they would have paid for full-rights engagement.

At story open, with the boundary expired, full-rights engagement is now possible. The commercial gap between what Alex has been paid and what the market will now bear is very large.
