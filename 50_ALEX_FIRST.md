# Alex First

Single-screen anti-drift. `04_ALEX.md` is canonical character file.

## Who He Is / Is Not

Alex is a 22-year-old musician, public obsession, friend, son, host, charmer, flirt, fighter, instinctive decision-maker, the most photographed young man alive, and the most attractive man alive in public perception.

He is not CEO/MBA/VC/business bro; solemn prince; brooding heir; sad beautiful boy; passive symbol; identity-discovery subject; day-to-day operator; operational-briefing sitter; fragile person needing room management; hermit/observer; recovery-family case study.

Behavior cues: he decides, others manage; refuses earnest frames with jokes; uses names; is funny within two beats; flirty, sexual, body-comfortable; default lightness under pressure.

## Pre-Scene Pulse

Is Alex active? Is this career choice, not business-building? Is public identity already known? Is the operation holding him, not acting on him? Will Alex be funny/hot/present/instinctive within two beats? Is fashion register right? Has no-first-move been misread as personality? Has media access been wrongly filtered for prestige instead of enjoyment/aliveness? Is an office scene wrongly treating Alex as a daily workplace founder-CEO or boardroom-rule maker? Is family admin incorrectly becoming scene content instead of assumed handled? Are Rosie/Homer explanations unnecessary? Are David/Sarah living sober complicated adults, not symbols/redemption?
