# Phoenix Hollow Full Detail

Use this for the Kentucky compound, Rosie/Homer home gravity, Solstice, and childhood infrastructure.

## Core Summary

| Field | Canon |
|---|---|
| Place | Phoenix Hollow |
| Location | Woodford County, Kentucky |
| Scale | about 500 acres |
| Acquired | 1970 by Rosie |
| Build-out | begins 1978 |
| Move-in | 1980, when Rosie stops touring |
| Story-open occupants | Rosie and Homer by default |
| Privacy | inside gate, no press/cameras |

Phoenix Hollow is not a Nashville/Opry/Bluebird adult-gig geography. It is home, studio, family seat, protective infrastructure, and cultural room.

## Purpose

Phoenix Hollow is the populated room Rosie and Homer built around Alex against the London world that took David and Sarah out of parenthood. It is private, full, lived-in, and unperformed.

The London co-residence years, 2014-2019, are the only sustained period Rosie and Homer were not primarily in residence. Alex's own Kensington years continue after they return to Phoenix Hollow.

## Approach And Grounds

White board fencing, private road, mature hardwoods, rolling land, paddocks, creek line, wooded hollows. The house appears gradually. It is built into the land rather than imposed on it.

## Main House

Fifty-plus rooms across three floors. Traditional Southern architecture filtered through old-money restraint: high ceilings, long corridors, deep wraparound porches, no performance.

### Ground Floor

Formal dining room seating twenty-four, drawing room, Homer's library, Rosie's study, family living room with fireplace, music room, breakfast room, small parlor.

### Second Floor

Alex's private east-wing suite, Rosie/Homer west-wing suite, and formal guest wing with four ensuite bedrooms.

### Third Floor

Two guest suites, staff quarters when Maria chooses to stay over, storage, attic room where old instruments live.

## Kitchen

Maria's domain. French Laundry / Per Se level skill served without ceremony at a Kentucky kitchen table.

Do not write her making generic Southern comfort food. Use serious, precise cooking: pastry, stocks, soft eggs, elegant seasonal dishes, braises, quiet technical competence.

## Music Room

1923 Steinway Model D, Rosie's piano. Soundproofed. This is where Alex first touched a piano. His hands know this action better than any other.

## Alex's Suite

Private east wing, roughly 3,600 sq ft. Residence within a residence.

| Room | Detail |
|---|---|
| bedroom | east-facing, pasture line, quiet, linen, dark woods |
| dressing room | suits, stage wardrobe, everyday clothes, shoes, watches, cufflinks |
| bathroom | marble/brass, freestanding tub, walk-in shower, heated floors |
| private lounge | sectional, fireplace, bar, good bourbon, Barolo, big screen, close friends at 1am |
| two guest bedrooms | ensuite rooms for closest people during compound stays |

No staff routes through Alex's suite. Security stops at the suite door.

## Studio

Separate building 2-3 minutes uphill from the main house. Purpose-built and soundproofed. Control room with Homer's Neve console, live room with Yamaha concert grand, isolation booths, lounge, kitchen.

This is where serious music work happens at Phoenix Hollow.

## Amphitheatre

Natural bowl shaped into the hillside. Stone terracing, grass seating, outdoor stage with professional rigging, live-event sound system, 500+ capacity. Annual Solstice ends here.

## Pool And Fitness

Separate building with 25m temperature-controlled lap pool, hot tub, full gym, yoga/movement studio with sprung floor, changing rooms, steam room.

## Story Rules

- No press inside the gate.
- Rosie/Homer are here at story open.
- Phoenix Hollow chat can include global cultural figures, but the physical compound scene only includes people actually placed there.
- Do not use Kentucky as shorthand for country music.
- Do not give Alex adult Nashville performances because Phoenix Hollow is in Kentucky.
- David is an occasional visitor when in from New York if Ryan places him. He is polite, awkward, sober, and not part of the default household rhythm.
