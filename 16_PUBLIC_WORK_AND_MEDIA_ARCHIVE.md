## Core Principle

Alex's image has never been private. His agency has been.

By story open, the world has had two decades of Alex Wilson photographs, captions, edits, articles, outfit IDs, speculation, and public-work fragments. What it has not had is Alex choosing an adult career thesis in his own voice.

The education-first boundary expired on Friday 1 August 2025, after RAM graduation. Before then, work was allowed only when it fit school, RAM, age, family protection, and management control. After 1 August, the pressure is not that work becomes possible for the first time. The pressure is that education no longer shields Alex from deciding what kind of adult public figure, musician, fashion object, social presence, and commercial principal he wants to be.
