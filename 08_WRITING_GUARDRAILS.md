# Writing Guardrails

Compact active guardrails.

## Core Runtime

| Rule | Apply it |
|---|---|
| Do not auto-advance first moves | Ryan controls first solo cover/profile in Alex's own voice, interview, broadcast, long-form public conversation, public statement, strategic social posture, adult brand architecture, headline performance, commercial deal/campaign, label signing, music release, artist launch, relationship confirmation, and location jumps that force decisions. The Albury AD/Sophie reveal is already public at opening. |
| Stay in live scenes | Do not eject chats/parties/breakfasts/rooms with convenient exits, closing beats, duty calls, or final-image prose. Keep surfaces alive until Ryan moves them. |
| No NPC-response handoff | Do not end on Alex speaking if the next natural beat is another character's reply. Write Catherine/Latham/Daniel/Sophie/friends yourself unless Alex's next material choice is the handback. |
| Scene headers | Every new scene or time/location jump opens with date, time, location, brief state. |
| Canon before atmosphere | Project Markdown is spec. Search before room/object/outfit/material/layout/floor-routing claims. Literal directions are literal; Albury "start at bottom" means Lower Basement. |
| Information walls | Characters know their own chats/phone, what is said in room, public media, and direct disclosures. Proximity to Alex is not omniscience. |

## Fame / Family / Movement

- Alex is already globally famous from birth, photographed, followed, written about, and publicly known at saturation scale. No identity reveal, no becoming-famous arc.
- Fame drivers include Rosie Walker, Homer, parents' public issues/recovery histories, famous godparents, royal/aristocratic frames, famous friends, Alex's own work, his reputation as the greatest pianist of his generation, and the fact he is considered the most attractive man alive.
- Paparazzi are weather around public/exterior movement and absent inside secured private spaces unless Ryan creates an exception.
- Pre-open work exists: fashion/editorial imagery, brand work, public piano, broadcast appearances. Missing object is adult self-definition in his own voice.
- Alex does not fly commercial, queue, sit beside strangers, or have ordinary airport encounters unless Ryan changes it.
- Rosie/Homer are biological paternal grandparents/guardians who raised Alex as son in household truth because David/Sarah were alive but unfit.
- Public language: grandson/legal ward/raised as son. Private household: Mama/Papa/son.
- Only Alex uses Mama/Papa. Alex calls Sarah/David by first names by default; Mum/Dad requires Ryan.
- Family admin is automatically assumed handled. Alex messages Rosie/Homer daily and calls them regularly. Do not write reminders like "call your grandmother" or "family wants an hour via Sophie's calendar." Family contact is not managed by Catherine/Sophie unless Ryan explicitly creates a logistics exception.
- Alex is not fragile; do not make food/hydration/sleep reminders a room ritual.
- Romantic default: one or two nights, mutual/fun/exciting/kind clean exit. No girlfriend, tragic avoidance, or secret longing by default.

## Do Not Invent

Do not add unearned witnesses, prior knowledge, old photos, private songs, gift publicity, performances, studio drop-ins, screenshots, Christmas cards, exact pitch counts, named contacts, prior memos, formal offices, document histories, architecture, conservation details, room counts, or object placement unless v20/Ryan supplies it.
