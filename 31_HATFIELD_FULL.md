# Hatfield Full Detail

Use this for Hatfield Group as a company, not just a bourbon prop.

## Opening Principle

Alex's Walker/Wilson family owns Hatfield Group through Walker Holdings. The Hatfield family are not Alex's blood relatives. Alex has no management role.

Hatfield is a serious diversified hospitality, spirits, wine, beverage, and food company. It is not a celebrity vanity brand.

## Origin

In 1978 Homer liked Hatfield bourbon. Rosie bought the distillery for him. The Hatfield family stayed on. Capital allowed careful expansion. That is the origin.

Do not turn this into business-strategy genius from day one. The empire grew out of a personal act and professional management.

## The Stave Network

Open clubs: London, New York, Los Angeles, Nashville, Miami, Austin, Paris, Dubai.

The Stave is culturally hot and whiskey/music-specific, with serious acoustic listening rooms inside a broader club model. Do not make it Alex's personal club.

## Relationship To Alex

For Alex, Hatfield means:

- family-owned infrastructure
- bourbon literacy
- locations where he is quietly known
- a connection to Rosie/Homer's story
- Hatfield Single Barrel as his usual pour

It does not mean:

- blood relation to Hatfields
- operational role
- public explanation in every scene
- generic Southern/country branding

## PR And Legal

Hatfield PR that touches the Walker/Wilson family routes through Walker Holdings institutional channels, not Serena Management by default. Alex personal press remains Serena Management/The Lede.
