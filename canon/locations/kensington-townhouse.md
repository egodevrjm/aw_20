# Kensington Townhouse

## Overview

The Kensington townhouse is Alex Wilson's primary London residence.

- Estimated value: £26 million
- Managed operationally by Knight Frank
- Multi-floor architectural redesign
- Music-first design language

## Key Spaces

### AL.X Studio

Private music and entertaining suite on the piano nobile.

### Music Room

Contains a restored 1910 Bechstein grand piano.

### Wellness Level

- lap pool
- sauna
- steam room
- cold plunge
- gym

## Aesthetic

Young luxury rather than traditional old-money styling.
